The eMotionBand is a bracelet that informs the user's emotional state of mind through the animation of preset coloured LED lights. The bracelet makes use of psycho-physiological sensors that monitors the Galvanic Skin Response (GSR) and the heart rate. The signals are interpreted and analysed real-time and sent to a mobile application where the user can see their status and more additional statistics on screen. The design of the bracelet will be based on the succesfully funded Kickstarter project Embrace+. The bracelet can take on the colors of the six basic emotions (Anger, Disgust, Fear, Joy, Sadness, Surprise) and can flash at the pace of your heartbeat. These colour settings can be customized in the settings panel of the mobile application. The eMotionBand will be able to alert you with visual and tactile cues like fast red flashes to warn you when you're too stressed or when your heartbeat is too high.

As already said the design of the eMotionBand will be based on the Embrace+. However the design needs to be adjusted to fit the psycho-physiological sensors. The band will be made of 100% silicona and will be available in different sizes (Small, Medium, Large). The bracelet will be waterproof and will be compatible with iOS and Android devices. The application can be downloaded in the Appstore and at Google Play. In order to manifacture the eMotionBand and develop the mobile applications we need help from our backers!
